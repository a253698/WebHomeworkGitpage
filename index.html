ABOUT ME


Hi, I'm Mavis, a 19 years old photography lover from Taiwan.

Chinese name, Chao-Chin Liu

I started photography since I was 12, and I fell deeply in love with it.
For me, taking photo is drawing with light and shadow. Camera is your brushes, mother nature is your paper.

Macro Photography is something that I love the most, finding detials in nature and life had become something that I cannot live without.

And this is a small online art gallery about my trip to Kyoto.
